Capabilities:  TMTS provides engineering, analysis and programmatic support to include conducting studies, independent analysis, technological assessment, and other related tasks in system definition, experiments, production, fielding and sustainment. TMTS assesses plans and projected/planned program costs, performance issues, scheduled risks, and formulates alternatives for risk mitigation and issue resolution. In addition, TMTS provides network and help desk operations support.

 

Capabilities Specific to ITS-SB:  TMTS will assist in planning, coordination and implementation of activities and duties in support of computer network and help desk operations to include hardware, operating systems and desktop/network applications. Support services will include maintenance of network routers and switches, administration of email systems, and providing troubleshooting, technical support, and information assurance.
