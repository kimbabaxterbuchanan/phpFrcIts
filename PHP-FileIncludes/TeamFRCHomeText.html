Future Research Corporation (FRC) has been chosen in a downselect as one of the 25 viable vendors in the Army’s 400 million dollar Information Technology Services - Small Business (ITS-SB) IDIQ contract. FRC is an award-winning, minority-owned, Small Disadvantaged Business that provides logistics and programmatic support; multimedia / video / distance learning support; manufacturing; information technology; and engineering services. FRC is well-versed in the latest technological developments and is poised to take advantage of the present and future technological climate. FRC is a high-energy, proactive, customer-oriented organization on the cutting edge of Advanced Technology and IT management. At FRC, we pride ourselves in our ability to provide our customers with Total Life Cycle Logistic Support and sustainability, while establishing and maintaining a trust-based partnership to provide the highest quality service and products available. Our Ultimate Goal:  to provide the Warfighters with the tools necessary to accomplish their missions. 

FRC has extensive experience in software engineering and technical application development. Our experience is in software development, software configuration management, IT infrastructure, testing and evaluation, and conversion and interface support. FRC provides process re-engineering assistance and expanded maintenance and support. Additionally, we are capable of providing turnkey tailored solutions in a wide range of applications development and software engineering environments. FRC has a broad experience base and the tools to provide outstanding service and support. For more information, please contact us at TeamFRC@gmail.com.

About ITS-SB

The Computer Hardware, Enterprise Software and Solutions (CHESS) Program, in coordination with the Army Contracting Agency (ACA), Information Technology, E-Commerce, and Commercial Contracting Center (ITEC4), is seeking qualified Small Businesses to support the Army enterprise infrastructure and infrostructure goals with IT services worldwide. The ITS-SB scope includes the Command, Control, Communications, Computers, and Information Management (C4IM) requirements as defined in Army Regulation 25-1 (AR 25-1). The IT services solutions are categorized in the task areas identified below.  

ITS-SB Task Areas
